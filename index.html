    WE WANT A FUTURE !

    And we want it now. We must act!

    WTF?! is a group of volunteers joining together with a simple but very hard mission: CLEAN THE PLANET.

    We lead many type of activities which raises awareness, improves the local environment or contributes to solving of an environmental problem.

    We gather every week to pick up the trash in our cities, towns and beaches, and to generate a change in our communities and politics.

    We also connect groups together to create a support network in their communities.

    “You cannot get through a single day without having an impact on the world around you. What you do makes a difference and you have to decide what kind of a difference you want to make.”

    — Jane Goodall
